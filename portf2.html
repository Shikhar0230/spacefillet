House Of Colors

Energy, Embodied. Why live in grayscale when the world exists in technicolor? House of Colors reimagines residential design by marrying Vastu’s elemental precision with a fearless love for pigment. Each space features a dedicated highlight color, replacing "neutral" with "natural energy." It’s a home designed to vibrate at the frequency of joy.
