Zest Garden

Zest Garden is designed as a lively and refreshing dining space that captures the energy and vibrancy suggested by its name. The design draws inspiration from the warmth of citrus tones and the freshness of greenery, creating an environment that feels bright, welcoming, and full of life. Warm orange hues, brass accents, and terrazzo textures are balanced with abundant indoor plants and open terrace seating, bringing together warmth and natural freshness throughout the café. Softly illuminated niches, layered lighting, and textured materials add depth and character to the space while maintaining a relaxed and social atmosphere. The result is a vibrant terrace café that embodies the spirit of “zest”—a place where color, light, and greenery come together to create an energetic and inviting destination for dining and gathering.

PROJECT INFORMATION


  • Location: Karnal, Haryana
  • Project Area: 3,000 sq.ft.
  • Project Type: Hospitality
  • Project Language: Warm Botanical
  • Project Scope: Interior Design And Execution
