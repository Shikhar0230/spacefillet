The Veiled House

This façade renovation project in Kurukshetra was designed to reinterpret traditional architectural elements in a refined and sophisticated manner. The design draws inspiration from familiar regional materials and forms while presenting them in a cleaner, more balanced composition. Terracotta-toned tiles and a patterned jaali screen become key features of the elevation, bringing warmth, texture, and a sense of cultural character to the home. These elements are complemented by soft stone finishes, simple columns, and layered architectural volumes that add depth and proportion to the façade. The interplay of earthy materials, natural textures, and carefully placed lighting creates a welcoming and timeless visual identity for the residence. The result is a façade that feels rooted in tradition while expressing a more polished and contemporary sense of craftsmanship.

PROJECT INFORMATION


  • Location: KURUKSHETRA, HARYANA
  • Project Area: 300 sq.yards.
  • Project Type: Residential
  • Project Language: Timeless Vernacular
  • Project Scope: ARCHITECTURAL DESIGN
