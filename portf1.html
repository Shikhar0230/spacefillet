Ivory Home

This apartment renovation project in Indirapuram was designed with the vision of creating a calm, modern, and minimal living environment. The design approach focused on clean lines, a neutral palette, and carefully selected materials that bring warmth and sophistication to the space while maintaining simplicity. Each area of the home was thoughtfully reimagined to improve functionality while keeping the overall aesthetic light and uncluttered. Subtle textures, warm wood finishes, and soft lighting were used to add depth without overpowering the minimal design language. The result is a balanced and elegant home that reflects contemporary living while ensuring comfort and practicality for everyday life.

PROJECT INFORMATION


  • Location: Indirapuram, Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh
  • Project Area: 1,800 sq.ft.
  • Project Type: Residential
  • Project Language: Modern And Minimal
  • Project Scope: Interior Design And Execution
