THE CLAY HOUSE

The Clay House is a residential interior project designed to create a warm and grounded living environment through a palette inspired by natural earth tones. Soft neutrals, warm textures, and carefully balanced materials come together to establish a calm and welcoming atmosphere throughout the home. The interiors emphasize simplicity and material warmth, with subtle architectural detailing, integrated lighting, and thoughtfully designed storage elements that enhance both functionality and visual harmony. The result is a refined and timeless home that reflects comfort, warmth, and a quiet sense of sophistication.

PROJECT INFORMATION


  • Location: Kailash hills, East of Kailash, Delhi
  • Project Area: 2,000 sq.ft.
  • Project Type: Residential
  • Project Language: Subtle Luxury
  • Project Scope: Interior Design
