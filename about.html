OUR STORY

Space Fillet is a design atelier driven by the pursuit of refined living. Led by architects Samridhi, Preeti, and Abhinav, the studio stands at the intersection of precision, artistry, and understated luxury.

Rooted in the belief that true luxury lies in detail, Space Fillet approaches every project with a meticulous lens — where each line, material, and proportion is intentionally crafted. Their design philosophy transcends trends, focusing instead on creating timeless spatial narratives that exude quiet sophistication. At Space Fillet, spaces are not merely designed — they are curated. Each project is an immersive journey, shaped through a deep understanding of the client’s lifestyle, aspirations, and identity. The studio seamlessly blends functionality with elevated aesthetics, resulting in environments that feel both indulgent and deeply personal. With a commitment to excellence and an uncompromising eye for detail, the trio delivers bespoke design solutions that redefine modern luxury. From intimate residences to expansive architectural expressions, every Space Fillet creation is a statement of elegance, intention, and enduring value.

At its essence, Space Fillet is not just about creating spaces — it is about crafting an experience of living that is distinct, timeless, and unmistakably yours.
